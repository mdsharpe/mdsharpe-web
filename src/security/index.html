Matt's security tips

  • Don't use the same password across multiple websites or apps

    Use a password manager to create and store long, strong passwords that you don't need to remember.

  • Don't click on links or attachments in emails you weren't expecting

    Scammers often use links and attachments to infect your computer with malware or viruses. Be cautious of any links or attachments from unknown sources.

  • Don't give away personal information

    Never give away your personal details, such as your bank account or credit card number, to anyone you don't know or trust.

  • Don't trust unexpected phone calls

    If you receive a call claiming to be from an organization e.g. a bank, don't start divulging details to them.

    Instead, take that person's details and tell them you'll call back.

    Then, once you're off the call, look up the organization's phone number and call them yourself.
